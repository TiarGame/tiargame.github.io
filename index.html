Welcome to T.I.A.R. - The Ultimate Heist Game!

Dive into the world of high-stakes heists in T.I.A.R. (This Is A Robbery!) – a thrilling action-packed game where you take on the role of a criminal mastermind. Plan and execute daring robberies at banks and properties to earn money and rise to the top of the criminal underworld. Inspired by iconic games like PayDay and One-Armed Robber, T.I.A.R. offers players an intense, strategic, and adrenaline-filled experience. Work with a team of skilled criminals or go solo – the choice is yours. From acquiring specialized tools for each job to customizing your look, every choice impacts your heist. Whether it’s infiltrating a high-security bank or robbing a luxurious estate, your plan will be your weapon, and time will be your enemy.
